## Background

I'm a large proponent of NixOS. Without exception, that's what I run on my systems and I've been extremely pleased. I've spun up dozens of different distros or platforms. Configuration and maintenance always turns into a burden.

Ultimately, NixOS just works. The main caveat is that you have to do the initial legwork and learn the new paradigm.
